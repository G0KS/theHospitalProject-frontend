Our Services

PHYSIOTHERAPY

Physical therapy also known as physiotherapy is provided by physical therapists who promote, maintain, or restore health through physical examination, diagnosis, management, etc

ECG

Ambulatory ECG. In this test you wear a small monitor which constantly records your heart rhythm. This test records the electrical activity of your heart

AUDIOLOGY

Audiologists use a variety of tests and procedures to assess hearing and balance function and to fit and dispense hearing aids and other assistive devices for hearing

ENDOSCOPY

An endoscopy is a procedure in which your doctor uses specialized instruments to view and operate on the internal organs and vessels of your body
